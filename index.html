Gibson Les Paul Faded 2018, Worn Bourbon

The Gibson Les Paul Faded Electric Guitar, Worn Bourbon (2018) delivers an incredible feel and tone coupled with the worn-out look of a guitar that's seen many performances. Its classic mahogany body and maple top feature a stunning worn bourbon finish, providing beautifully sweet, warm characteristics and an organic aesthetic. Its slim taper neck and ultra-modern weight relief ensures players receive the ultimate comfort and a smooth performance, while the rosewood fingerboard helps enhance the guitar's highs and lows for an incredibly versatile play. Its PAF inspired humbuckers deliver beautifully vintage tones, with the 490R and 490T offering a moderately high output suitable for a wide range of musical styles. The Gibson is also equipped with vintages-style keystone tuners and a tune-o-matic bridge to enhance string stability and resonance. Delivered with a softshell case for protection, the 2018 Les Paul Faded delivers modern Gibson craftsmanship with a classic style and tone.Product Ref: 96163
